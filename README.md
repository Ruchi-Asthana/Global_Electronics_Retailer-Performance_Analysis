# Global Electronics Retailer Performance Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

## Project Overview:

This project focuses on designing an end‑to‑end Power BI analytics solution for a global electronics retailer.
The goal is to transform raw transactional, product, and customer data into interactive dashboards that uncover 
sales trends, product performance, customer behavior, and strategic business insights.
The project involves performing a complete data analysis lifecycle—data cleaning, modeling, DAX calculations, 
visualization, and insight generation—while converting each analytical requirement into actionable components of the dashboard.

## Key Actions & Analytical Objectives

* Calculate core business KPIs including total sales, total profit, total quantity sold, total orders, products unique purchasedand average order value.

* Build month‑wise sales performance visuals to identify seasonal trends.

* Identify top 5 best‑selling products and bottom 5 lowest‑selling products to support inventory and merchandising decisions.

* Determine the highest revenue‑generating product category and compare sales vs profit across all categories.

* Analyze regional/state‑wise sales performance to highlight strong and weak markets.

* Identify top 5 customers by total purchase amount and evaluate customer purchasing patterns across regions.

* Segment customers into High, Medium, and Low value groups based on total spending and analyze their contribution to revenue.

* Perform year‑wise sales growth analysis to understand long‑term performance trends.

* Calculate category‑wise percentage contribution to total sales to assess category concentration.

* Detect regions with high sales but low AOV to highlight high-frequency customer engagement as well as flag low profit margin per transaction .

* Create a KPI dashboard summarizing Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Orders, and other key metrics.

* Identify the busiest sales month and busiest sales day to support operational planning.

* Develop a complete Sales, Product and Customer Analysis Dashboard covering sales, profit, margins and category trends.

* Add slicers and filters for Region, Category and Year to make the dashboard fully interactive.

* Generate actionable business insights based on trends observed in sales, customers, products, and regions.
